# Easy CTF 2 – Suspicious Email

An employee reports an email that looks like it came from Microsoft. The email warns that the user’s mailbox is full and requires immediate action.

---

## Observation

Key details from the email:

- Sender: `support@m1crosoft-mail.com`
- Link text: “Verify your mailbox”
- The message creates urgency and threatens email shutdown

---

## Question

Which **phishing indicator** is the strongest sign this email is malicious?

---

## Flags (Choose One)

- **A)** Urgency in the message  
- **B)** Suspicious sender domain  
- **C)** Use of company branding  
- **D)** Formal language  

---

Correct Flag: **B**
